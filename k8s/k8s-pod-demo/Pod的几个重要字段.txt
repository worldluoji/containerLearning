1. NodeSelector
apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
...
spec:
 nodeSelector:
   disktype: ssd
意味着这个Pod只会运行在携带了 disktype: ssd标签（Label）的节点上

2.NodeName:该字段被赋值，说明该POD已经被调度过

3.hostAliases
apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
...
spec:
  hostAliases:
  - ip: "10.1.2.3"
    hostnames:
    - "foo.remote"
    - "bar.remote"
...
其实就是/etc/hosts文件定义的域名-IP映射关系
